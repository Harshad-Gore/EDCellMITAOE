ABOUT EDF

Entrepreneurial Development Foundation

The Entrepreneurial Development Foundation (EDF) at MIT Academy of Engineering is dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership among students and faculty by providing resources, mentorship, and hands-on opportunities.

What We Do

  • Promote entrepreneurial culture and mindset.
  • Organize workshops, seminars, and competitions.
  • Support student startups and innovative projects.
  • Connect students with industry mentors and investors.
  • Facilitate funding and incubation opportunities.

Our Vision

To be a leading center for nurturing entrepreneurial talent and innovation, empowering students to become successful entrepreneurs and leaders.

Our Mission

  • To create an ecosystem that encourages creativity and risk-taking.
  • To provide guidance, mentorship, and resources for aspiring entrepreneurs.
  • To bridge the gap between academia and industry through collaborative initiatives.
  • To foster social responsibility and ethical business practices.
OBJECTIVES

Our Objectives

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Instill entrepreneurial thinking and problem-solving skills among students.

Skill Development

Conduct training programs and workshops to enhance business and technical skills.

Startup Support

Provide incubation, mentorship, and funding support for innovative ideas.
